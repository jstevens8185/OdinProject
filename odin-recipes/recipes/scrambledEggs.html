Scrambled Eggs

Description

These eggs are unlike anything you’ve ever experienced before. The typical eggs found in the United States are overcooked and dry. But not these eggs!

This dish is remarkably easy to make and will have you going back to this recipe every time!

Ingredients

The ingredients needed are as follows:

  • 3 Large eggs
  • 1 Table spoon of milk or heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tea spoon of dill weed
  • pinch of salt
  • pinch of pepper
  • 3 slices of butter

Steps

  1. Heat skillet to low heat with butter until butter is melted
  2. While skillet is heating, crack eggs in bowl and scramble well
  3. Once butter is heated, add eggs to skillet and stir constantly
  4. When eggs start to thicken add milk or whipping cream
  5. When eggs start to thicken again add salt, pepper and dill weed
  6. Remove eggs when they reach an oatmeal consistency
