Biscuits and Gravy

Description

No one should ever go without this southern breakfast delacacy: buiscuits with sausage and egg gravy.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of 8 flaky butter milk biscuits
  • 1 pound of Jimmy dean hot or maple breakfast sausage
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 2 cups of milk
  • Fresh pepper grinder
  • 1/2 stick of butter
  • 5 hard boiled eggs

Directions

  1. Cook Sausage in large skillet
  2. Melt butter in with cooked sausage
  3. Sprinkle flour into sausage while stiring until there are no clumps
  4. Put biscuits into pre heated oven
  5. Slowly add milk to sausage and flour mixture. Try not to cool off the pan too quickly
  6. Slice eggs and add to pan
  7. Grind pepper into gravy until your arm hurts from grinding pepper
  8. Stir on low heat until desired thickness
  9. Serve biscuits topped with sausage and egg gravy
