Fried Rice

Description

This egg fried rice slaps at parties or by yourself. It is quick and easy to make as long as you have a rice cooker.

Ingredients

  • Cup of white rice
  • 4 large scrambled eggs
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • Teaspoon Garlic
  • Teaspoon salt
  • Teaspoon pepper
  • Table spoon vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Cook rice in rice cooker according to directions specified by your rice cooker
  2. Heat oil in large pan to med-High heat
  3. Add Rice, soy sauce, and seasonings to pan and stir often
  4. Add cooked eggs to rice and stir for 1 min on heat
  5. Remove from heat and serve
