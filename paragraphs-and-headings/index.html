How to learn guitar for noobs

So you want to learn guitar, huh? Well you've come to the right place. Sit down, grab a drink, and buckle up because this is going to be a bumpy ride. Oh? You don't drink? Suit yourself. I'll have an extra for you.

Prepare yourself.

Before we can begin the journey of becoming a guitarist there are some things we must consider.

The most important of these being this: YOU MUST OWN A GUITAR!

I can't even begin to explain how many have sought me out only to be turned away because they thought that I would give them the tools they need. How silly of them to assume such things!

Yes, you must own (or perhaps rent) your instrument. Only then can we move on to requirement number two: KNOW WHY YOU WANT TO LEARN GUITAR!

Everyone has their own reason for learning. It may be to get more ladies. It may be to win your fathers approval. My reason was because I got grounded when I was 12 years old and a guitar was the only thing left in my room for a month. It doesn't matter what your reason is as long as you have one.
